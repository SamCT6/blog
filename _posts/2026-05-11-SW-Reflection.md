The key componets of this website involve an application database for applicants, a check in page for community members, and multle databases for admin to use to manage different aspects of the food bank. This project involved learning new skills to be able to better help our community partners. One of things we worked on was prioritization, picking which project aspects are crucial to project impkementation and which aspects can be used later. One task that I worked on that I learned alot about was authentication and role managment.  


I feel like the approach of social change employed by our community parter is very effective. Our partner have to balance alot of buercratic duties through administration of the food bank as well as necesarry forms to be able to recieve support from the state. Through this website the ease of which our partners can balance these task is greatly improved. This will allow them to imporve their outreach and provide easier, more accesable support to our community members,


My fundamental values, social ideals, or significant beliefs have not really been challeneged but I have seen the challenges that doing work like our community partners face. I see the importance in people doing the work we did to help people better help those in need. It shows that help isnt just volunteering but also supporting those who volunteer and the impace that that can have.
